Philips Hue

With the Philips Hue project, we had to think of a way to use Philips Hue lamps in a practical or fun manner, and then program it like that. I and two friends had the idea to make a lamp that displays the battery percentage of your laptop. We made the code in Javascript and used HTML to make a website where you could use it and adjust the brightness.

VR-Game

I together with a friend of mine decided to start working on a VR-Game in unity. We thought of this because our school had just bought two new VR/Render PC's and we thought it would be fun to make something to test them out with. We started with the basics, such as teleportation and being able to grab stuff. And we eventually tried to make a working gun. We are now trying to program NPC's that come after you and attack you if you come to close.
