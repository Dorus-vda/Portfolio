Me


Hello,

I'm Dorus van den Akker and I'm a Dutch 3rd-year student at the Metis Montessori Lyceum. I was born in the city of Amsterdam and I have lived here all my life. At my school, we have a special program called Coderclass. In this program, we learn the basics of all sorts of computer science, from animating and modelling with Blender to coding programs in Python. We use a badge system to check if students learn the required skills. This means that when you finish a so-called module, you will get a badge for that module. With every three or four badges that you get, you will ascend to the next level. And at the end of year three, you should have earned all your badges and you will specialize in a specific aspect of computer science.
